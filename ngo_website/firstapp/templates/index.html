Our Mission

WHO WE ARE

Support Way Worldwide saves and transforms the lives of the poor and vulnerable all over the globe. We lead special surgical missions and run a network of innovative solar-powered maternal and infant health clinics across Europe. We also provide life-changing scholarships to young female victims of terrorism and gender inequality.
