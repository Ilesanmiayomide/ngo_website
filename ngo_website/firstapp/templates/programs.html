Our Programs

Transforming Lives

SIX PILLARS OF IMPACT

Every program we run is designed to create lasting, sustainable change — not just short-term relief. From world-class surgical care to education and mental health, Support Way Worldwide addresses the root causes of vulnerability and empowers communities to thrive long after we leave.

Program 01

MEDICAL MISSIONS

Our flagship program organises free surgical missions that bring board-certified orthopedic surgeons and medical specialists directly to underserved communities. Focusing on hip, knee, and bone surgeries, we give patients their mobility — and their lives — back.

  • Over 3,400 free orthopedic surgeries performed
  • Multi-national teams of volunteer surgeons
  • $12M+ in donated medical equipment & supplies
SUPPORT THIS PROGRAM
Program 02

MENTAL & MENSTRUAL HEALTH

Mental health stigma and lack of access to menstrual hygiene resources remain critical barriers to women's wellbeing in many of the communities we serve. Our programs educate, destigmatize, and provide vital supplies to keep girls in school and women healthy.

  • Community mental health education workshops
  • Distribution of menstrual hygiene kits to students
  • Trained local health advocates in 12+ communities
SUPPORT THIS PROGRAM
Program 03

MOTHER AND CHILD

Our network of solar-powered maternal and infant health clinics brings prenatal and postnatal care to communities where none previously existed. Staffed by trained local midwives and supported by international medical advisors, these clinics have already helped over 8,200 women deliver healthy babies.

  • 8,200+ healthy deliveries supported
  • 15,000+ women & children screened for anemia
  • 100% solar-powered clinic infrastructure
SUPPORT THIS PROGRAM
Program 04

SCHOLARSHIPS

Education is one of the most powerful tools for breaking the cycle of poverty. We provide full and partial scholarships to young children who are victims of terrorism, conflict, and discrimination — giving them the chance to build a future on their own terms.

  • Full & partial scholarships to secondary and tertiary institutions
  • Priority for all children regardless of thier race
  • Mentorship and career support included
SUPPORT THIS PROGRAM
Program 05

SUPPORT FOR SCHOOLS

We equip under-resourced schools with computers, books, laboratory supplies, and teacher training — transforming classrooms into environments where every student can achieve their potential. Our STEM Leadership Summits inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators.

  • Computers, textbooks, and lab equipment donated
  • Annual STEM Leadership Summits for students
  • Teacher professional development training
SUPPORT THIS PROGRAM
Program 06

HEALTH SYMPOSIA

Our annual health symposia bring together medical professionals, policy makers, community leaders, and international experts to share knowledge, develop strategies, and build the networks needed to improve healthcare delivery across underserved regions.

  • Annual multi-day symposium with expert speakers
  • Cross-sector collaboration between healthcare & government
  • Published policy recommendations & research findings
