How It Began

OUR STORY

Support Way Worldwide was founded on a powerful conviction: that quality healthcare and education are human rights, not privileges. What began as a series of volunteer surgical missions has grown into a global network of life-saving programs touching thousands of lives each year.

Today, our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, educators, and community advocates work tirelessly across Europe and beyond — running innovative solar-powered maternal health clinics, funding scholarships for young women affected by conflict, and delivering world-class surgical care to communities with no other option.

Every program we run, every life we touch, is made possible by supporters like you — individuals, corporations, and foundations who believe in a world where no one is left behind.

Our Mission

To save and transform the lives of the poor and vulnerable through high-impact surgical missions, maternal and infant healthcare, scholarships for young women, and educational programs — delivered with compassion, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

A world where every person — regardless of geography, income, or circumstance — has access to quality healthcare and education. We envision self-sustaining communities empowered by knowledge, health, and hope.

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, and excellence guide everything we do. We believe in honoring the dignity of every individual we serve, maintaining the highest standards of accountability, and relentlessly pursuing greater impact with every dollar entrusted to us.

The People Behind The Work

OUR LEADERSHIP

Our board and leadership team bring decades of experience in medicine, public health, education, and international development to guide Support Way Worldwide's mission.

Dr. A. Onyema

Founder & Executive Director

A board-certified orthopaedic surgeon and passionate humanitarian, Dr. Onyema founded Support Way Worldwide to bridge the gap between world-class medical care and those who need it most.

Dr. M. Adeyemi

Medical Director

With over 20 years of surgical experience across three continents, Dr. Adeyemi leads our medical mission teams and oversees the clinical standards of all Support Way Worldwide health programs.

Mr. J. Okafor
